Meet again at summit of the transmission tower

Play at Cluster

It is a game in which "worker" and "foreman" with different roles work together to ascend a huge power transmission tower.

The "worker" climbs the tower while receiving instructions from the "foreman", and restores power by pulling the power lever.
The "foreman" cannot get out of the elevator, but can help the "worker" by showing them invisible platforms.

Even if you are separated from, call each other and recover "energy" to the city!
※Must be played by 2 or more players.

best visual at Cluster Game Jam 2020 Winter

  • Genre: Coop action game
  • Release Date: 2020-12-20
